Nearby Attractions – Must Visit Places

Thalupulamma Talli Temple

The Sri Talupulamma Ammavari Temple in Lova, commonly known as Thalupulamma Lova, is a Hindu temple in Lova, East Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh, India.

Annavaram Satyanarayana Temple

Located on Ratnagiri Hill, this sacred temple offers divine blessings and panoramic views of the lush Godavari valley.

Mango Orchards

Tuni is famous for its mango production. The orchards offer a scenic retreat during the summer and are vital to the local economy.

Vakalapudi Beach

Just a short drive from Tuni, this tranquil beach offers a perfect escape with its gentle waves, golden sands, and peaceful surroundings.
